STAFF

Devin Brown, LPC-S, LAC, NCC

Founder of Brown Counseling

Licensed Professional Counselor-Supervisor

Devin Brown is the Founder of Brown Counseling & Consulting, LLC. Devin earned a Bachelor of Arts in 2012 from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. He then received his Master of Counseling Education in 2016 from Southeastern Louisiana University. Devin started working at  Woodlake Addiction Recovery Center in 2016. He was then promoted to Program and Clinical Director from 2017 – 2020 at the Woodlake Abbeville, LA location. Devin started working at Our Savior’s Church in 2020 helping with counseling and recovery ministries. Devin sees things from a Psychodynamic and Biblical perspective (when applicable). Devin is an active member and serves on the Louisiana Counseling Association as an Awards Committee Co-Chair. He is also on the Southeastern Louisiana University Advisory Council.

Devin decided to create a set of mental health journals, filled with prompts to give people a way to creatively write. His journals are on sale and can be sold through this website. Devin is now a Licensed Professional Counselor Supervisor, supervising new provisionally PLPCs, and enjoys spending time building up the employees of Brown Counseling & Consulting, LLC. One goal is to branch Brown Counseling to different parts of Louisiana and in other states. As well as opening up the consulting side, by helping other counseling practices become beneficial to servicing their communities. One fun fact about Devin is that he enjoys going to New Orleans Saint’s game!

KEYONIA IGLUS, M.S. PLPC

Provisionally Licensed Professional Counselor

Keyonia Iglus is a Provisionally Licensed Professional Counselor at Brown Counseling. Keyonia earned a Bachelors of Arts in 2002 from Dillard University. She then earned a Master of Science degree in Psychology from Purdue Global University in 2017. She also completed post graduate hours, earning a certification in addiction counseling at Post University in 2020. As well as training in Multi-systemic therapy, an intensive multi tactical treatment, assessing the impact of individual and extended systems on unhealthy behaviors. Keyonia services multicultural communities with a client focus ranging from teen to senior adult. “My mission is to prepare individuals, or groups/families to be confident in their resources, new expertise, and reliable support team to manage improved behaviors. I am drawn to this industry by my passion for learning and sharing ways to inspire myself and others. It is my duty as a professional to gain and distribute the knowledge needed to develop quality of life. I really look forward to seeing your growth throughout this journey.”

Therapy

Therapy is a process where the counselor and client can talk about any life event that has caused a change in the daily routines of life. From traumatic events like divorce, death…etc, to subtle life events like a change in jobs or buying a home. I see therapy as a place to gain insight on bettering your quality of life. From a psychodynamic perspective, I look at past events and see how it ties into your life today. In this process, my goal is to collaborate with you to help gain the necessary coping skills to add to your tool belt of life.

I specialize in helping couples or individuals overcome anxiety, depression, low self-esteem, life transitions, perfectionism, and relational issues. Also, for the last 5 years, I have specialized in helping clients focused on addiction or trauma come to a healthier place by aligning with the goals of the client.

Therapy is a collaborative process. Taking the first step to schedule a session may be intimidating or create anxiety. I encourage you to reach out for a free consultation today.
